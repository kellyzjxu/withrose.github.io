Be BOLD

by: Hana

I've always loved wearing black, but now that I've moved to NYC, I feel like it's literally all I wear.

The top two photos are by fashion blogger Wendy Nguyen from Wendy's Lookbook. I love how they are both "all black" looks, but include accents that make the outfits pop. I've really been loving the look of lace lately because they add a little something to every outfit without being too over-the-top. I also love the bits of gold in her outfits: the gold watch in the first photo, the gold platform on her shoes in the second. They stand out and catch our eye against the all black canvas that the rest of her outfit provides.

The third photo is a much more simple approach to the all black outfit. Even here, there is contrast, though not with colors. Her shiny leather jacket contrasts with the rest of her outfit and adds interest to her otherwise plain outfit.
