Be BOLD

by: Hana

First things first, I love black. Maybe it's because I've always dreamed of being a New Yorker. (I mean.. Are you really a true New Yorker if you don't wear all black?)

I love how streamlined and effortlessly professional you look when you wear all black. But to avoid looking boring, try adding a little pizzaz to your outfit! For example, these black lace pants with the white underlay are super fun and cute! In addition, cutouts are super in right now - and it's a good way to peep a little skin without it being too scandalous.

Incorporating different materials is a great way to spice up a monotone look! Pair a leather jacket with some chiffon for an edgy but sweet look.

What do you think? Is all black the answer?
